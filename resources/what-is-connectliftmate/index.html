What is Connectlift Mate?

Connectlift Mate is a lightweight Chrome Extension designed to help you simplify your LinkedIn journey. It saves your time, organizes your outreach, and eliminates the boring copy-paste chaos.

💭 Why Was It Built?

We all know the pain of typing the same messages repeatedly on LinkedIn connection notes, follow-ups, templates scattered across Notion or Docs. Connectlift Mate was born out of this everyday frustration.

✨ So, What Does It Do?

  • 📌 Save your best LinkedIn notes & messages
  • ⚡ Insert them in one click directly into LinkedIn
  • 💬 Reuse templates without switching tabs
  • 🔍 Boolean Search Builder for LinkedIn targeting
  • 🗃 Export/Import your data easily

🔐 No Login. No Tracking. No Bloat.

This extension doesn’t ask for your data. Everything is stored locally no sign-up, no privacy headaches, just you and your workflow.

🎯 Who Is It For?

  • 👩‍🎓 Students doing outreach
  • 💼 Job seekers trying to stand out
  • 🧠 Coaches, founders, and freelancers using LinkedIn
  • 👩‍💼 Professionals nurturing new connections
